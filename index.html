About Us

Emma Robinson is a highly regarded, professional beautician who specialises in beauty make-up. Her signature look, best described as fresh and flawless skin with a radiant glow makes her indispensable to models, brides and everyday women alike.


Emma has an extensive background in the fashion and beauty industry. She has been plying her skills for over 20 years. Emma lay her career foundations with CIBTEC and CIDESCO qualifications in beauty therapy from the Bronwyn Conroy School of Beauty.


Emma first gained recognition working at the renowned ‘Transformations’ Beauty Studio in Lisburn, and her clientele included local TV and modelling personalities. After 5 years working and further studying in Lisburn, Emma returned to her native Fermanagh to open her own salon in 2005.


Emma is extremely knowledgeable about the make-up and beauty industry and keeps in touch with the latest make-up and beauty trends, techniques and products. In 2012 Emma became fully qualified in reflexology and holistic massage, specialising in the treatment of cancer patients.


Emma is based in in Tempo, County Fermanagh, and works out of her relaxing home make-up and beauty studio. While being completely private in a dedicated modern treatment room, you can relax in the stress free surroundings and enjoy the attention that makes each visit special.

How to find us

From Tempo:

From the centre of town, take the B80 signposted for Brookeborough

Travel 2.6 miles and Emma’s Beauty Parlour is on the right, and signposted at the driveway entrance

From the A4 Belfast Road:

Turn right signposted for Stonepark Baptist Church

After 200 feet, turn left onto Stonepark Road

Travel 2.3 miles and turn right signposted for Tempo (3 miles)

In half a mile, Emma’s Beauty Parlour is on the left, and signposted at the driveway entrance
