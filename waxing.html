Waxing

Waxing is a complete hair removal solution suitable for both men and women. People tend to enjoy the benefits of waxing because they last longer than some other types of hair removal.

Waxing pulls hair from the root, which means the hair takes longer to grow back and, eventually, stops growing altogether. Also, waxing helps remove dead skin cells along with unwanted body hair. It’s like getting a quick exfoliation every time.

Your skin stay hair-free longer with waxing, and your hair thins out over time. If you shave, the hair only gets thicker and grows back rapidly. The more you shave, the more you have to shave to keep it up!

Emma's Beauty Parlour uses the Harley waxing range of waxing products. You can download the Harley waxing product guide for full details of the products used during your treatment, and tips and tricks for before you arrive at the salon to ensure your maximum enjoyment of the experience.
